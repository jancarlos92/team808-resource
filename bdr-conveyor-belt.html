Team Reference · How Your Book Actually Works

The BDR
Conveyor Belt

Your book of business is not a warehouse — it's a production line. Accounts come in, get worked, get resolved, and move out. If the belt stops moving, the pipeline starves.

The Line: Four Stations

EVERY ACCOUNT PASSES THROUGH ALL FOUR
STATION 01

Ingest

Prospect and load new accounts onto the belt. Right vertical, right size, right systems footprint. No intake, no output.

▶
STATION 02

Process

Work the account through outreach: calls, sequences, LinkedIn. Multi-thread the finance office until you get a real answer.

▶
STATION 03

Resolve

Every worked account lands in one of three outcomes: Not Now, Qualified, or True No. "No answer yet" is not a resting state.

▶
STATION 04

Digest

Move resolved accounts off your active belt — into a nurture plan or out of the book entirely. Dead weight slows the line.

FLOW DIRECTION: INGEST → DIGEST · THE BELT RUNS EVERY DAY

Station 03 — The Three Outcomes

EVERY REAL ANSWER SORTS INTO ONE LANE
Lane A · Not Now

Right fit, wrong timing

Interest exists but the timing is off — mid-budget cycle, contract not up, priorities elsewhere. This account is warm inventory, not a loss.

NEXT MOVESet a dated follow-up task. Log the reason and the re-engage trigger. Keep light-touch warming until then.
Lane B · Qualified

Book the discovery call

Pain confirmed, authority reachable, willingness to talk. This is the whole point of the belt — convert the conversation into a QDC.

NEXT MOVELock the QDC on calendar before you hang up. Confirm attendees, send the invite, prep the AE handoff.
Lane C · True No

Genuinely disqualified

Recently implemented a new GL system, or zero interest in switching or even learning about one. Respect the no — it's information, not failure.

NEXT MOVELog the disqualifier (system + go-live date if known). Route to long-term nurture or remove. Free the belt space.

The Three Daily Motions

ALL THREE, EVERY DAY — NOT ONE PER DAY
Every day

Prospect

Feed the belt. New accounts in.

  • New accounts added 15
  • New contacts sequenced 20
  • Research blocks 2 × 30 min
Every day

Warm

Keep the belt moving. Touches out.

  • Dials 80
  • Emails / sequence steps 25
  • LinkedIn touches 10
  • Live connects 5+
Every day

Capitalize

Take the output. Convert what's ready.

  • QDCs booked 1 / day pace
  • Weekly QDC target 3–5
  • Follow-ups closed out 100%
